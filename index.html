Free Pomodoro Timer with Exercise Breaks - Stay Focused & Active

Movemoro is a free online pomodoro timer designed for students and office workers who want to boost productivity while staying healthy. Our study timer combines the proven Pomodoro Technique with movement breaks to keep you focused, active, and energized during long work or study sessions.

Why Use a Pomodoro Timer?

The Pomodoro Technique is a scientifically-backed time management method that breaks work into focused 25-minute intervals. This productivity timer helps students ace exams, office workers complete projects, and anyone looking to improve focus and reduce burnout.

Perfect Study Timer for Students

Students love Movemoro as their go-to study timer. Combat study fatigue with quick movement breaks between focus sessions. Perfect for marathon study sessions, exam prep, homework, and late-night cramming while keeping your body and mind fresh.

Office-Friendly Productivity Timer

Designed for desk workers and office environments. Our work break timer includes exercises that don't require floor contact or equipment - perfect for professional settings. Reduce sedentary time, improve circulation, and boost energy without leaving your desk.

Key Features

  • Customizable focus timer (default 25-minute work sessions)
  • Quick desk exercises to unlock breaks (30-90 seconds)
  • Optional longer exercises to extend break time
  • Office-friendly movement breaks (no floor required)
  • Study-optimized themes and settings
  • No sign-up, completely free to use
  • Works on desktop and mobile devices

How It Works

  1. Focus: Work or study for 25 minutes with zero distractions
  2. Move: Complete a quick exercise to unlock your break
  3. Rest: Enjoy your earned break and recharge
  4. Extend: Do a longer exercise to add bonus break time
  5. Repeat: Build focus stamina and stay healthy
